Forza Horizon 6 Rumors Point to a Japanese Setting

While there has been no official announcement from Microsoft or Playground Games, recent credible rumors have sparked excitement among racing fans about the future of the Forza Horizon series. The most persistent and widely circulated speculation points to a **Japanese setting** for the next installment, *Forza Horizon 6*.

Fans have long requested a return to an urban and mountainous setting, and a Japan-based map would offer the perfect blend of neon-lit cities, winding mountain roads, and stunning natural landscapes. Reports suggest that an official announcement could be just around the corner, possibly as early as **September 2025**.

The game is rumored to be a major visual upgrade, leveraging the advanced graphics engine from *Forza Motorsport*, and is expected to be an Xbox and PC exclusive, launching day one on Xbox Game Pass. With the development team reportedly receiving additional support from Turn 10 Studios, all signs point to an ambitious and stunning racing experience.
